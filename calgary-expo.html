Calgary Expo 2022

The Calgary Expo, also known as the Calgary Comic and Entertainment Expo, is an annual convention held in Stampede Park every year in late April. The main focus of the convention is on comics, gaming, movies, and TV shows though it encompasses a wide variety of fan media. Voice actors, comic book creators, and other creators and celebrities come in from around the world to attend and various cosplay competitions are held, with the con being kicked off with the POW parade on Stephen Avenue. Aside from retail sellers, hundreds of small creators and individual artists set up booths in Artist Alley with products including art prints, jewelry, pins, plushies, sculptures, and much more. Fan content dominates the majority of the artwork sold, but plenty of artists sell original works here too. It's a great place to meet local, independent artists who make amazing work, and the community is super warm and welcoming; it's a great place to see people's creativity shine and meet some friends!
