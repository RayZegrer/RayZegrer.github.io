Job Shadowing and Volunteering

Job Shadowing

Clinics in Calgary I've job shadowed at:

Silverado Sports Physiotherapy
• Weekly shadowing over the summer of 2023; learned about various muskuloskeletal injuries, the underlying mechanisms, and some of the treatments for them.
Rahman Orthotics
• Watched the casting and then subsequent fitting of two knee orthotics.
Cascade Prosthetic Services
• Intro to a prosthetic clinic and the general work flow surrounding prosthetic production, materials, types, etc.
Colman Prosthetics and Orthotics
• Intro to clinic operations, patient care/appointments, and the production of foot orthotics.

Volunteering

YMCA (2019)
• Week of running games at the YMCA summer camps for children ages 6-12
Calgary Public Library (2017-2019)
• Volunteered at various events, including Canada Day, duct tape crafts, and a 6 week math help program for elementary kids.
UofC Open House (2022, 2023, & 2024)
• Volunteered at the kinesiology booth for the UofC Open House
Calgary Special Olympics 2024
• Worked venue takedown and Healthy Athletes - FUNFitness which was health screening for the athletes; the station I worked at tested for flexibility and range of motion at the hip, knee, ankle, and shoulders.
CP Kids and Family (2024)
• Took a course in adapted bike fitting for children and volunteered for a day as an assisted bike fitter which included selecting and adjusting adaptive bikes to fit the child's needs.
Science Fundamentals (2024-2025)
• Travelled to an elementary school to show science demonstrations to a grade 4 class. Demos were chosen by the teacher and ranged from finger printing for criminal science to clouds in a jar for weather.
